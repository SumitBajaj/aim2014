WHY ATTEND

WHAT IS AIM ?

AIM (Aspire, Inspire, Motivate) is an annual conference, that a few passionate and enterprising IT professional working in vibrant startup cultures have been organizing since year 2011.

WHY ATTEND AIM ?

This conference aims to encourage creativity, ideation and to nurture the culture of continuous learning within our teams. This is one day when our teams take time off from their daily work and listen to tales of inspiration from folks from different walks of life. They then go back to see how they can inculcate similar values in their personal and professional lives.
